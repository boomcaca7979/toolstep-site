## Selection Methodology

Every candidate keyword was scored using the ToolStep Priority Score model:

```
Priority Score = (Search Demand × 0.30) + (Buyer Intent × 0.30) + (Affiliate Value × 0.20) + (Competition Inverse × 0.20)
```

The first 100 pages were selected by applying these filters:

1. **Prioritize:** High purchase-intent keywords ("best", "review", "under $X")
2. **Prioritize:** High affiliate-value products ($100+ Amazon items)
3. **Prioritize:** Google-friendly long-tail keywords (lower competition)
4. **Avoid:** Overly broad keywords ("best keyboard")
5. **Avoid:** Zero-buyer-intent keywords ("history of mechanical switches")
6. **Avoid:** Products with no commercial affiliate value
