### 4.3 Long-Term Avoid
- Head-to-head with Wirecutter on "best office chair" (domain authority gap)
- Head-to-head with Tom's Hardware on "best gaming keyboard" (benchmark authority gap)
- Broad single-word keywords ("best keyboard", "best mouse")

### 4.4 ToolStep's Defensible Moat
1. **Long-term testing:** 60-180 day testing durations vs competitors' 1-2 week tests
2. **Internal link density:** Review ↔ Best List ↔ Comparison triangle that competitors lack
3. **Niche depth:** "Best X for Y" (programming, back pain, tall people) where competitors are thin
4. **AI Tools + Developer Tools:** Emerging categories where traditional publishers are slow
